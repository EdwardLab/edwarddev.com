Edwarddev.com ("we", "our", "us") uses cookies to improve your experience on our website. This Cookie Policy explains what cookies are, how we use them, and your choices regarding cookies.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit a website. They help the website recognize your device and remember information about your visit.

2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for the following purposes:

  • Google Analytics: We use Google Analytics to collect and analyze information about how our website is used. This helps us improve our website's functionality and performance. The information collected is anonymized and cannot be used to identify you personally.
  • Theme Preferences: We use cookies to store your website theme preferences locally on your device. This allows us to remember your theme choice when you return to our website.

3. Your Choices Regarding Cookies

If you prefer to avoid the use of cookies on our website, you can disable cookies in your browser settings. Please note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality and features of our website. Here are links to manage cookie settings for popular browsers:
